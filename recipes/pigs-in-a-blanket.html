pigs in a blanket


Description

pigs in a blanket is a dish primarly made for children, that invloves wrapping hot dogs in dough and then baking them in the oven.
pigs in a blanket can me made in different sizing options, adittional ingridients to add more flavour, different types of dough,
and can, if you so choose to experiment, even different types of meat.

ingridients list:

  • Dough (amount and type of dough will be up to you) (if you do not how to make dough, pls view )
  • hot dogs(amount and type of hot dog or similiar products will be up to you)
  • optional ingridients

cooking steps:

  1. prepare all of the ingridients on the table
  2. preheat oven to 175 C
  3. seperate dough into smaller parts, and then reshape them into 7.5 cem diameter circles
  4. add your additional ingridient of choice and your hotdog equivalent on top of the dough circle, and then roll it around
  5. place your pigs blankets in the oven on a cookie sheet
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until the dough is golden brown, about 10 to 15 minutes
  7. dig in!
